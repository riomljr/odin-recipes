The perfectly season and cooked scrambled eggs

How to cook the perfect half scrambled eggs everytime

Unlike traditional fully scrambled, half scrambled eggs taste great. And you can tell them apart from powdered eggs.

Ingridients

  • 3 eggs (pasture raised preferrably)
  • butter
  • black pepper
  • Coarse sea salt
  • cheese(optional)

Steps

  1. heat pan to medium/high (more towards medium)
  2. Place butter on pan and spread it around evenly
  3. Once butter is fully melted, crack eggs one at a time on pan
  4. let the eggs sit for about a minute, let them cook
  5. sprinkle some black pepper (as much as you want)
  6. sprink a generous "pinch" of coarse salt
  7. once the egg whites seems to have cooked, scramble the eggs
  8. as as soon as you scramble the eggs turn off the heat
  9. let them finish cooking with the residual heat for about 30 seconds
  10. serve and add cheese (optional)
