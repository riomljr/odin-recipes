How to make the best mushroom pasta

Let's make the best mushroom pasta! It's fun and easy and the outcome is ahh-may-ziiing!

Ingridients

  • mushrooms (1/2 cup)
  • butter
  • olive oil
  • Coarse sea salt
  • pepper
  • smoked paprika or regular
  • tomato paste
  • 1/2 onion or whole
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • parmaggioano cheese
  • garlic powder
  • 1/2 package of pasta (your choice)

Steps

  1. make pasta per package inst.
  2. while pasta is cooking, in another pan on medium high heat, place butter and olive oil
  3. Once butter is fully melted add chooped mushrooms, onions
  4. cook until onions are slightly brown
  5. add cream, and 2 tbs of tomato paste
  6. add a sprinkle of salt
  7. add 1 tbls of each ingridient left
  8. stir stir stir unitl its a creamy reddish color and reduce to a simmer
  9. once pasta in done, save 3/4 cup of water and add sauce along with cheese
  10. stir stir stir until it's all mixed and serve hot
