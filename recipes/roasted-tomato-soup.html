How to make one of the best roasted tomato soups

Let's make a roasted tomato soup! It's pretty straight foward and easy but the taste is amazing. Just follow the steps towards a delicious reward.

Ingridients for the main dish

  • 8 Tomatoes
  • 1 large red onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 head of garlic
  • salt
  • black peper
  • olive oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • smoked paprika
  • cumin (optional)
  • dill
  • 2 cups of choice of broth

Ingridients for the garnish

  • heavy whipping cream
  • olive oil (OR coconut milk instead)
  • honey
  • dill

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350F degrees roast
  2. arrange all the veggies onto a baking pan inlcuding full garlic cloves
  3. drizzle olive oil on top
  4. sprinkle generous pinches of coarse salt and pepper
  5. throw the bay leaf on top
  6. sprinkle smoked paprika genrously on top
  7. sprinkle a tiny pinch of cumin (optional)
  8. Once oven is ready, roast for 30-45 mins
  9. Once roasted, add all ingridients to a blender except for the garlic
  10. add two cups of broth
  11. squeeze garlic out of casks into blender
  12. blend away until it's all the same consistency
  13. strain the soup using a cheese cloth or something similar
  14. in a seperate cup add the garnish ingridients together
  15. add some chopped dill
  16. serve soup and add garnish on top and enjoy!
